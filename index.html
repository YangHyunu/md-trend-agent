MD Trend Agent

AI-powered Fashion Trend Research Platform

About

MD Trend Agent is an AI-powered trend research platform that helps fashion merchandisers discover emerging products, colors, materials, and consumer interests from publicly available trend signals.

The project analyzes trend data from authorized sources to support product planning and market research.

Features

  • Fashion Trend Discovery
  • Pinterest Trend Analysis
  • Visual Content Research
  • Product Planning Support
  • AI-assisted Trend Summaries
